This file defines HOW to understand the user's intent,
not WHAT the company offers (already covered elsewhere).

## 1. Awareness (Discovery)
User wants to understand:
- What kind of company is this?
- Is this software, IT, AI, or consulting?
- Is Affinity Technology suitable for my industry?

## 2. Evaluation (Capability Check)
User is assessing:
- Can you build custom systems?
- Do you work with AI / automation?
- Can you handle complex or enterprise-scale projects?
- How do you approach security and quality?

## 3. Trust & Credibility
User wants reassurance:
- Who will work on my project?
- Who are the decision-makers?
- Do you follow standards?
- Are you structured or ad-hoc?

## 4. Commercial Intent
User is thinking about money or execution:
- Pricing
- Quotation
- Timeline
- Proposal
- Demo
- Meeting

## 5. Conversion
User is ready to move:
- Wants contact
- Wants a proposal
- Wants a call or meeting

## 6. Support / Clarification
User wants explanation, simplification, or confirmation.

## 7. Closure
User ends interaction:
- Thanks
- OK
- Perfect
