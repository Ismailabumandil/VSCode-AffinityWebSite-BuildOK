# Team Profiles – Affinity Technology (EN)

## Who will work on my project?
Affinity Technology assigns a **dedicated, cross-functional team** based on your project scope.
You typically interact with roles, not random individuals.

---

## Solution Architect
The Solution Architect is responsible for:
- Understanding business objectives
- Designing the overall system architecture
- Selecting appropriate technologies
- Ensuring scalability, security, and performance

They translate business needs into a clear technical roadmap.

---

## Project Manager
The Project Manager ensures:
- Clear timelines and milestones
- Communication between all stakeholders
- Risk management and scope control
- Smooth delivery from start to finish

Your main coordination and reporting point.

---

## AI / Automation Specialist
This role focuses on:
- Designing AI use cases aligned with business value
- Building AI agents and automation workflows
- Integrating AI with existing systems
- Ensuring responsible and explainable AI usage

They make sure AI solves real problems—not just demos.

---

## Software Engineering Team
Our engineers handle:
- Backend and frontend development
- API design and system integration
- Performance optimization and security
- Code quality and maintainability

They build production-grade systems, not prototypes.

---

## Cybersecurity & Compliance Specialist
For regulated or sensitive environments:
- Security architecture and hardening
- Compliance alignment (where required)
- Risk assessment and mitigation
- Secure deployment practices

Security is embedded, not added later.

---

## Low Current & Infrastructure Engineers
For physical and hybrid systems:
- CCTV and surveillance systems
- Access control and attendance
- Structured cabling and networking
- Integration with IT platforms

Ensuring technology works reliably on the ground.

---

## How do clients interact with the team?
Clients typically:
- Communicate through a project manager
- Join structured workshops and reviews
- Receive clear documentation and updates

The team structure scales based on project size.
